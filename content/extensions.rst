Extensions
==========

.. image:: /static/extentionIcon.png

RoboFont is highly customizable by installing extensions.

Recommended: Mechanic
---------------------

`Mechanic <http://www.robofontmechanic.com>`_ from `Jack Jennings <http://ja.ckjennin.gs/>`_ is a RoboFont extension managing open sourced and `githubbed <https://github.com/>`_ extensions.
